Before you get started, what is Plane Sailing?

Plane Sailing is the revolutionary DevOps accelerator pack to bootstrap and streamline both culture, technology teams and business goals to deliver value quickly from day one!

Plane Sailing does this by providing,

  • A Core Platform dubbed "Plane Sailing Core" - the only platform to build your services
  • A DevOps Accelerator Pack - a collection of materials packed full of succinct and practical information that will guide you through what you need to do . Both video material
To be clear, the Plane Sailing technology stack is not and will not be a market place or a self-service control plane.
It will be an extremely optimised and finely tuned turnkey solution for bootstrapping clusters for production workloads.

Culture

The Accelerator Pack includes modern DevOps culture practices, ideologies and ways of working to evolve the traditional IT mindset and motivate your teams. We're working on aggregating and gamifying engineering metrics from multiple technologies to motivate teams.

Security Focus

Security is an after thought in smaller teams - often a skills gap or a lack of implementation time. Not with Plane Sailing! You get a secure stack right out of the box with each tool pre-configured with security in mind.

Feedback Loop

All aspects of the Software Development Lifecycle are considered from the Observability Framework all the way back to a developers workstations' git hooks. A prime example of how Plane Sailing accomplishes 'shift-left' and closing the infamous feedback loop.

Cost Effective

The Plane Sailing environment consists largely of Open Source tools. This means it is optimised for financial cost but also grows the Open Source community.
